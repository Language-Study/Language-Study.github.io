Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

What this app stores

Language Study stores account and learning information you provide, such as your email address, vocabulary entries, skill progress, portfolio items, and settings preferences.

How data is used

Your data is used only to provide core app features, including signing in, saving your progress, syncing your content across devices, and enabling mentor view features when you choose to use them.

Third-party services

Language Study uses Firebase Authentication and Firestore to manage sign-in and securely store app data. If you sign in with Google, Google account details required for authentication are processed by Google and Firebase.

Data sharing

Your data is not sold. Data is only shared when required to run services (for example, Firebase infrastructure) or when you explicitly enable mentor access features.

Your choices

You can update your account email, reset your password, and remove app content from within the app. If you delete your account, your authentication access is removed.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated over time. When updates are made, the date at the top of this page is revised.
